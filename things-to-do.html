Things to Do in Medellín

Your curated guide to the top parches across Medellín's most popular zones.

Best parches in El Poblado

  • Walk around Parque Lleras and Provenza for food and nightlife.
  • Visit local design stores and galleries in Manila.
  • Enjoy brunch and sunset views from rooftop restaurants.

Best parches in Laureles

  • Explore La 70 for live music and local bars.
  • Try neighborhood cafés and bakeries near Primer Parque.
  • Join a salsa class and experience Medellín's dance culture.

Comuna 13 guide

  • Book a guided walking tour to understand local history.
  • See street art murals, escalators, and viewpoints.
  • Support local artists through food and handmade goods.

Outdoor activities

  • Take the metrocable to Parque Arví for nature trails.
  • Hike Cerro de las Tres Cruces early in the morning.
  • Visit Jardín Botánico and nearby public parks.

Coffee shops worth visiting

  • Specialty coffee tasting in El Poblado.
  • Cozy remote-work cafés in Laureles.
  • Traditional Colombian coffee experiences downtown.
